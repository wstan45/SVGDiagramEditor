Your diagram editor app goes here!

Select a shape on the right hand side. Then click and drag on the left hand side to draw the selected shape.

To link shapes together, click on the text 'link' of one shape and double click on another target shape to link 2 shapes together.
Note: once you've clicked the text 'link' of one shape, you can double click any number of other target shapes to connect that one shape with all of those targeted shapes.

If you do not wish to connect that shape with other shapes anymore, double click on the text 'link' to disable the generation of links of that shape with other shapes.
